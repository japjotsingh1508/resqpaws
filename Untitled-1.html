Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly does ResQ Paws respond to reports?

Our system processes reports instantly and notifies the nearest NGO immediately. Response time depends on the NGO's availability, but most cases receive attention within 1-2 hours.

Is there any cost to use ResQ Paws?

No, ResQ Paws is completely free for users reporting stray animals. Our platform is supported by donations and NGO partnerships.

How accurate is the AI in assessing animal conditions?

Our AI has been trained on thousands of animal health cases and achieves 92% accuracy in identifying common injuries and conditions. However, final assessment is always done by the rescue team.

Can I track the status of my reported case?

Yes, once you create an account, you can track all your reported cases through your personal dashboard and receive updates on the rescue progress.

How ResQ Paws Works

1. Reporting Process

When you encounter a stray animal in distress, simply use our platform to report it. The process is quick and easy:

  • Take or upload a photo of the animal
  • Provide location details (or use automatic GPS detection)
  • Describe the animal's condition
  • Submit the report with one click

2. AI Processing & Analysis

Our advanced artificial intelligence system immediately processes each report:

Image Analysis

Detects injuries, breed, and approximate age

Severity Classification

Prioritizes cases based on urgency

Location Mapping

Pinpoints exact coordinates for rescue

NGO Matching

Finds the most suitable rescue organization

3. Rescue & Follow-up

Once the report is processed, our system ensures swift action:

The nearest available NGO receives an immediate alert with all case details, including the animal's photo, location, and AI assessment.

The NGO dispatches a rescue team equipped with the necessary information to locate and safely secure the animal.

The case is tracked through our digital dashboard from rescue to rehabilitation, with updates available to the reporter if they provided contact information.
