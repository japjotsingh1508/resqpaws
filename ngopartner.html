Efficiency Tools

Digital case management reduces paperwork and improves operational efficiency.

Community Network

Partnership Process

  1. Submit Application

    Complete the form with your organization details.

  2. Verification

    Our team will review your application and verify details.

  3. Onboarding

    Successful applicants will receive training on our platform.

  4. Go Live

    Start receiving alerts and managing cases through ResQ Paws.

Requirements

  • Registered animal welfare organization
  • Minimum 1 year of operational history
  • Dedicated rescue team
  • Veterinary partnerships or in-house medical facilities
  • Willingness to adhere to our operational guidelines
