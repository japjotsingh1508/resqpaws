Our Rescue Process

Discover how ResQ Paws leverages technology to make street animal rescue faster and more efficient

1

Report Received

A concerned citizen reports a stray animal in need through our platform, providing photos and location details.

2

AI Analysis

Our artificial intelligence processes the report, analyzing the image to assess the animal's condition and classify the urgency level.

3

NGO Alert

The system automatically notifies the nearest available NGO with all case details and precise GPS coordinates.

4

Rescue Dispatch

The NGO team is dispatched to the location with appropriate equipment based on the case details.

5

Treatment & Care

The animal receives necessary medical treatment and care at the NGO facility or partner veterinary clinic.

6

Case Management

The case is tracked in our system, with updates available to the reporter and NGO for follow-up.
